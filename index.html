Roronoa Zoro

SANTORYU OGI

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" (Kaizoku Gari no Zoro) is a swordsman and combatant of the Mugiwara Pirates and a former bounty hunter with a bounty of 320,000,000 berry, which is the 4th highest in the crew, after Luffy, Jinbe, and Sanji.
